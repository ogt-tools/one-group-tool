How to use

SimCompanies has 3 economic phases: Recession, Normal, and Boom. Economy shifts every Friday.

  • Production: Phase affects production speed (higher in Boom).
  • Retail: Phase affects demand volume (very high in Boom).
  • Strategy: Use history to predict shifts and time your expansions.
